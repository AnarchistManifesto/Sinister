(National Sentinel) SO Classified: During Thursday’s fiery hearing on Capitol Hill between members of the House Judiciary Committee and FBI official Peter Strzok, Rep. Louie Gohmert, R-Texas, dropped a bombshell that had not yet been reported in the media.

He said that Hillary Clinton’s emails sent through her private, unsecured server  a finding that was discovered by the Intelligence Community Inspector General (ICIG).

What’s more, Gohmert said, the FBI was notified of said finding but never followed up on it.

Recall that the agent in charge of the Hillary email probe was…Peter Strzok.

As reported by The Daily Caller:

The Intelligence Community Inspector General (ICIG) found an “anomaly on Hillary Clinton’s emails going through their private server, and when they had done the forensic analysis, they found that her emails, every single one except four, over 30,000, were going to an address that was not on the distribution list,” Republican Rep. Louie Gohmert of Texas said during a hearing with FBI official Peter Strzok.

“It was going to an unauthorized source that was a foreign entity unrelated to Russia,” he added.

Gohmert said the ICIG investigator, Frank Rucker, presented the findings to Strzok, but that the FBI official did not do anything with the information.

Strzok acknowledged meeting with Rucker, but said he did not recall the “specific content.”

“The forensic examination was done by the ICIG and they can document that, but you were given that information and you did nothing with it,” said Gohmert during his questioning of Strzok.

The Texas lawmaker further noted that someone told Michael Horowitz, the Justice Department Inspector General, about the issue, but that he “never returned the call.” He said Horowitz’s office was contacted four times.

There’s more.

The ICIG has previously discovered problems involving Clinton’s server that were ‘missed’ by the bureau.

As The Daily Caller previously reported, the FBI failed to notice that some of Clinton’s emails were clearly marked classified with a “(C)” at the time they were sent.

Which, of course, means Clinton .

More from The Daily Caller:
