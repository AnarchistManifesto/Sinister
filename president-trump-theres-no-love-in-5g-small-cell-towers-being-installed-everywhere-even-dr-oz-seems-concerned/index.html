President Trump, There’s No Love In 5G Small Cell Towers Being Installed Everywhere — Even Dr. Oz Seems Concerned

World

Written by: 0x000216

May 12, 2018

By B.N. Frank

President Trump is known for making many colorful and sometimes insensitive statements.  Despite this, he still occasionally speaks very freely about creating new policies based on love.

There is no love involved with elected officials passing state and federal laws that allow telecom companies to install 5G small cell towers all over the U.S. including in front of homes, in public rights of ways, in historical districts, and pretty much everywhere else.  Many Americans have been fighting these bills.

From the late George Carlin:

The word bipartisan usually means some larger-than-usual deception is being carried out.

In regard to new technology, for 20+ years, The Federal Communications Commission (FCC) along with both Democrats and Republicans have been responsible for creating, promoting, and passing legislation that limits municipal control over cell tower and other wireless infrastructure placement in American communities.

Current FCC Chair Ajit Pai has been pushing 5G.  President Trump has promoted 5G.

Former FCC Chair Tom Wheeler also pushed 5G.  Former President Obama also promoted 5G.

A few weeks ago, Dr. Oz featured a segment on his show about 5G small cell towers and technology.  He seemed to be concerned.  The citizens he interviewed were concerned.  The only medical journalist he interviewed seemed to be more concerned about legally covering her fanny than the health ramifications of 5G small cell towers being installed all over the country.

The CTIA keeps promoting 5G as a race that the U.S. needs to win.  This ain’t high school.  This is wireless (WiFI) microwave radiation-emitting cell towers being installed in front of people’s homes and everywhere else so Americans can binge-watch, surf, and post online at higher speeds than what we currently have.  Gag me with a spoon.

Some don’t agree with The Beatles’ sentiment that “All We Need is Love.”

But it’s hard to disagree with this:

“And in the end, the love you take is equal to the love you make.”

There is no love in 5G.  There is only loss.  It’s like living a bad dream. You’re probably as tired reading about it as I am writing about it.
