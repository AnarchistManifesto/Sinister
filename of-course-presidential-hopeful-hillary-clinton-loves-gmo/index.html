But it’s also interesting to see how some of these same media outlets supporting Hillary would alternately decry genetically modified foods…even though the former Secretary of State is all about it.

But let’s be clear, Hillary has been a professional corporatist her entire career. One of her first gigs was working for Rose Law firm, a prestigious Arkansas firm with big business clients the likes of Tyson, Walmart and yes, of course, Monsanto.

Interesting how in, say, the Huffington Post you’ll see article after article espousing the dangers of GMO foods — to not only us and the environment — and even a report about how Obama is signing orders to protect the GMO industry.

But you’ll also see seemingly contradictory cutesy, low-information pieces like “5 Reasons Why Hillary Clinton Is the Best Person for the Job,” which reads less like actual valid reasons someone should be president and more like something a little kid who has yet to develop critical thinking skills would write.

So GMO is bad for a bajillion reasons, but we need to vote in a pro-GMO president who will pave the way for the proliferation of, you guessed it, MORE GMO? You don’t think it’ll be Hillary in there signing orders to protect GMO next? Give us all a break.
