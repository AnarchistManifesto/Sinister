Finally: World has had enough of George Soros

Liberty

Written by: 0x000216

May 14, 2018

(NationalSentinel) Billionaire Hungarian-born financier George Soros, who has funded just about every Left-wing cause and organization under the sun, may have finally overplayed his activism.

In the wake of Donald J. Trump’s convincing electoral win over Democratic nominee Hillary Clinton, the world has figured out that Americans–and America–is through catering to the whims of traitors and globalists who have nothing good in mind for their country, top among those people being Soros.

As a means, perhaps, of ingratiating themselves to the U.S. and its new populist leader, Hungary, and other nations throughout the European Union, plan to clean out Soros-funded organizations and activist groups.

As reported by Bloomberg:

Hungary plans to crack down on non-governmental organizations linked to billionaire George Soros now that Donald Trump will occupy the White House, according to the deputy head of Prime Minister Viktor Orban’s party.

The European Union member will use “all the tools at its disposal” to “sweep out” NGOs funded by the Hungarian-born financier, which “serve global capitalists and back political correctness over national governments,” Szilard Nemeth, a vice president of the ruling Fidesz party, told reporters on Tuesday.

“I feel that there is an opportunity for this, internationally,” because of Trump’s election, state news service MTI reported Nemeth as saying. Lawmakers will start debating a bill to let authorities audit NGO executives, according to parliament’s legislative agenda.

Orban, the first European leader to publicly back Trump’s campaign, has ignored criticism from the European Commission and U.S. President Barack Obama’s administration for building a self-described “illiberal state” modeled on authoritarian regimes including Russia, China and Turkey. In 2014, Orban personally ordered the state audit agency to probe foundations financed by Norway and said that civil society groups financed from abroad were covers for “paid political activists.”

‘Illiberal’ in this sense is code for not Left-wing, of course, which is more authoritarian than any government Orban–or Trump–plan to run.

For his part, the president-elect has also singled out Soros for his political treachery:

Trump also accused the 86-year-old billionaire of being part of “a global power structure that is responsible for the economic decisions that have robbed our working class, stripped our country of its wealth and put that money into the pockets of a handful of large corporations and political entities.”

In the U.S., Soros-funded organizations have fielded activists who are directly responsible for ginning up violence and protests that have cost cities and taxpayers millions of dollars. In response, Soros has been allowed by the Obama administration to pretend as though he’s aloof and had nothing to do with any of it, all while getting away with sedition and other treasonous acts.

Now, his home country is putting the kibosh on his operations, because they are well aware of what his goals are: Overthrow of governments and endless revolution, with him sitting atop all of it playing the puppeteer/mastermind.

Prediction: Other countries will follow, either because they are simply tired of the interference or because they, too, will feel the need to ingratiate themselves to Trump’s America.
