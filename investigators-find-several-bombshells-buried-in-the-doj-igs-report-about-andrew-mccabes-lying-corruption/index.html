Investigators find several bombshells buried in the DoJ IG’s report about Andrew McCabe’s lying, corruption

Liberty

Written by: 0x000216

May 12, 2018

By J. D. Heyes

(National Sentinel) While Justice Department Inspector General Michael Horowitz’s report documenting serial lying by now-fired FBI Deputy Director Andrew McCabe is only 39 pages long, it is actually very revealing in terms of the level of corruption that existed within the Obama administration’s federal law enforcement establishment.

As reported by The Gateway Pundit, which reviewed the findings of some Internet sleuths who have combed through the report, emails found on a laptop belonging to top Clinton aide Huma Abedin’s ex-husband, former U.S. Rep. Anthony Weiner, D-N.Y., may be key to blowing several scandals wide open.

The news site noted that the IG’s report doesn’t have a lot to say about emails on Weiner’s laptop “other than to mention that a meeting was scheduled to get a search warrant to request the emails on October 27th, 2016” — which coincides with then-FBI Director James Comey’s email to Congress the following day that he was reopening the Hillary Clinton email investigation. 

But per text messages between FBI agent Peter Strzok and his lover, FBI lawyer Lisa Page, the bureau knew about the emails a full month earlier, in September 2016, and sent a team of agents to New York to review them.

Working off existing public information and after examining the IG report, independent investigators “have put together some astounding implications from [the] report when put in context with events at the time and Weiner’s email,” The Gateway Pundit reported.

Here is some background and the investigative findings:

— Weiner was sentenced to nearly two years in prison in September 2017 for sexting minors. Weiner and Abedin settled their divorce out of court.

— When the NYPD began investigating Weiner’s crime of sexting minors in 2016, they obtained his computer that one source said included as many as 650,000 emails that allegedly were related to corruption, pay-for-play and other heinous crimes that implicated several individuals including Bill and Hillary Clinton and other Left-wing elites. Some believe this material was Weiner’s “insurance” in case he ever needed it. 

— McCabe was told about Weiner’s emails in 2016 but he sat on the information for a month (McCabe’s wife, you may recall, is a Democrat and ran an unsuccessful campaign for a Virginia legislative seat). After about a month, someone — in the FBI or the NYPD — was growing impatient at the inaction regarding the emails found on the Weiner laptop. It is suspected that some who knew about them put pressure on the FBI to move on them.

— In late October, according to the IG’s report, McCabe and then-Attorney General Loretta Lynch, who had met with Bill Clinton on an airport tarmac in Arizona just days before Comey exonerated his wife, held a call with agents in New York’s field office. The subject of the call was leaks regarding an NYPD case involving the death of an unarmed black man, Eric Garner. — On Nov. 4, four days before the 2016 election, Blackwater founder Erik Prince — whose sister is Betsy DeVos, President Trump’s education secretary — made some shocking revelations about what FBI and NYPD investigators discovered in the Weiner laptop emails. 

“Because of Weinergate and the sexting scandal, the NYPD started investigating it. Through a subpoena, through a warrant, they searched his laptop, and sure enough, found those 650,000 emails. They found way more stuff than just more information pertaining to the inappropriate sexting the guy was doing,” he told Breitbart Radio. 

As an aside, Comey — in his Sunday interview with ABC News to hype his new book — confirmed that “hundreds of thousands” of emails were discovered on the Weiner laptop, which differs from what legal watchdog Judicial Watch reported in December, that the organization had been told only 72,000 emails existed.

In addition, investigators found State Department emails as well (Hillary, recall, was Obama’s secretary of state until 2013). In addition to those emails, investigators found “really damning criminal information” that includes money laundering and Hillary’s trips to a “sex island with convicted pedophile Jeffrey Epstein.” Earlier reports noted that former President Bill Clinton made frequent trips with Epstein to the island.

“The amount of garbage that they found in these emails, of criminal activity by Hillary, by her immediate circle, and even by other Democratic members of Congress was so disgusting they gave it to the FBI,” Prince said, adding that the NYPD said it would take the findings public if the bureau did not reopen its Clinton server probe.

Prince also dropped this bomb:

I believe – I know, and this is from a very well-placed source of mine at 1PP, One Police Plaza in New York – the NYPD wanted to do a press conference announcing the warrants and the additional arrests they were making in this investigation, and they’ve gotten huge pushback, to the point of coercion, from the Justice Department, with the Justice Department threatening to charge someone that had been unrelated in the accidental heart attack death of Eric Garner almost two years ago

Now the FBI-Garner team makes sense, along with Lynch’s heated, threatening remarks. It appears as though the Obama AG and Justice Department were using the Garner investigation as a way of shutting down the probe into the Weiner laptop (and remember, he and Abedin decided on an out-of-court, non-public settlement).
