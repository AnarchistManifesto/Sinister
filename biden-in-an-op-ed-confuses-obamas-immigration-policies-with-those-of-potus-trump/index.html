Biden, in an op-ed, confuses Obama’s immigration policies with those of POTUS Trump

World

Written by: 0x000216

September 7, 2019


By Jon Dougherty

(NationalSentinel) Former Vice President Joe Biden, who for some reason is leading the 2020 Democratic presidential lottery in most polls, published an op-ed in the Miami Herald on Monday in which he clearly mistook immigration policies enacted during his and President Obama’s administration for POTUS Donald Trump’s policies.

“Under Trump, there have been horrifying scenes at the border of kids being kept in cages, tear-gassing asylum seekers, ripping children from their mothers’ arms — actions that subvert American values and erode our ability to lead on the global stage,” Biden — or, more likely, a ghostwriter — noted.

“At a time when the challenges we face demand a united, regional response, Trump repeatedly invokes racist invective to describe anyone south of the Rio Grande, including calling migrants ‘animals.’”

The woman-and-child-groping former Veep also called out POTUS Trump’s “morally bankrupt” policies regarding Latin America while presenting his immigration plan as an example of “America’s values.”

Clearly, Creepy Joe is confusing his former boss’ policies and events that happened during Obama’s tenure with those of the current administration.

For example:

— The ‘kids in cages’ lie is a whopper. As documented by CNN at the time, migrant kids were literally ‘caged’ in 2014, when Obama-Biden still ran things.

— Trump is ‘separating families?’ No, actually, President Trump ended that practice. Obama used it, though, and a lot. And while it may not have been Obama’s policy, to begin with, as the Washington establishment press loves to remind everyone, the same media refuses to challenge Biden over this outright lie.

— Did POTUS Trump really call all migrants from Latin America “animals?” Hardly. The president reserved that very appropriate term for the vile murderers and rapists of the international gang MS-13, but The New York Times and the rest of the discredited establishment media concocted the lie and then perpetuated it. And again, Biden wasn’t challenged over it (and isn’t going to be by the ‘mainstream’ press).

Worse, Biden gladly met with Venezuelan dictator Nicolás Maduro four years ago. The Obama administration blocked sanctions against Maduro’s government.
