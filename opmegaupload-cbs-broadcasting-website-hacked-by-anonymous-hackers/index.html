#OpMegaUpload – CBS Broadcasting Website Hacked by Anonymous Hackers

Technology

Written by: 0x000216

May 17, 2018

Anonymous Hackers claim to hack CBS Broadcasting television network website and to have deleted the whole sever, deliberately leaving it with only one file “foundry.html”.  According to Anonymous this hack is a part of their #OpMegaUpload.

 

However, the website is back on but this is the biggest hack attack on any media platform after the last week hack of  Warner Music Group, Motion Picture Association of America and Universal Music Group’s website. Anonymous hackers are also carrying out attacks on Brazilian websites where today they defaced official website of  Tangara da Serra city.
