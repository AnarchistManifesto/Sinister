Section 13.1 on page 13 is medical and scientific ‘code’ portending that the HPV vaccine Gardasil® is NOT safe:

Medicine and science should know that too, I offer. Furthermore, it probably is the manufacturer’s way of getting around trying to address those concerns while they have not done proper studies to conclude long-term carcinogenesis, mutagenesis and impairment of fertility studies—even on rats—I suggest.

What kind of cockamamie science does the U.S. FDA/USDA/EPA buy into, anyway? Where is “evidence-based”—not consensus—science at work at U.S. government agencies? Are those agencies in corporations’ and lobbyists’ back pockets?
