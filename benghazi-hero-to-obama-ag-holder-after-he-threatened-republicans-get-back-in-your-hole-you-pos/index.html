Benghazi hero to Obama AG Holder after he ‘threatened’ Republicans: ‘Get back in your hole, you POS’

Liberty

(National Sentinel) War of Words: Last week former Obama Attorney General Eric Holder issued a veiled threat to Republicans in Congress who have been pushing the Justice Department to open a second special counsel investigation into alleged bias and corruption within Robert Mueller’s team.

“Speaking on behalf of the vast majority of the American people, Republicans in Congress be forewarned: any attempt to remove Bob Mueller will not be tolerated. These are BS attacks on him/his staff that are blatantly political-designed to hide the real wrongdoing. Country not party,” Holder tweeted.

The tweet and the threat it appears to contain did not sit well with former Army Ranger Kris ‘Tanto’ Paronto, one of those who survived the hellish Benghazi surprise attack in 2012 in which terrorists killed four Americans including U.S. Ambassador to Libya Christopher Stevens.

“Weren’t you the only Attorney General in U.S. history to be held in contempt of congress?? And you don’t speak for me or the vast majority of the American people you POS!! Get back in your hole rat. #FastandFurious #corrupt#Liar #ericholderhaszerointegrity” Paronto tweeted in response.
