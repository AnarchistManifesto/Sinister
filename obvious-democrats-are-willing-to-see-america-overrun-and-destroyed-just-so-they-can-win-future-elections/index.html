(National Sentinel) Sanctioned Invasion: In recent days President Donald Trump tried to give Democrats something they said they wanted: A more compassionate policy regarding the separation of migrant children from their accompanying adults when they are caught crossing illegally into the United States.

Trump kept his “zero-tolerance” policy in place which says that all illegal border crossers will be prosecuted, per the rule of law. But when and where children could remain with adults — if they were determined to be legitimate family members — the Trump administration would accommodate that.

The ink was barely dry on the executive order when Democrats and their sycophantic supporters in the establishment media began complaining that wasn’t good enough either. 

As we reported:

The pretend media has whipped up a frenzy among millions of Americans by creating a fake “child migrant crisis” at the U.S. border. But on Wednesday, POTUS Trump agreed to relax his policy of separating children from illegal adult border jumpers.

Naturally, the fakestream media wasn’t satisfied. Because like the Democrats they serve, they want nothing less than open borders. 

“Under the zero-tolerance policy … so long as these parents are being prosecuted criminally, they will continue to be separated from their children and will be separated from their parents for at least a few days,” said Natalia Cornelio, a Left-wing criminal justice reform director, in an interview with MSNBC.

Democratic lawmakers chimed in as well. Sen. Tammy Baldwin of Wisconsin whined that the order didn’t “reverse” the zero-tolerance policy (which again is based on enforcing the law as written). Sen. Dianne Feinstein agreed and then accused Trump of next plotting to “eliminate basic protections” for people seeking asylum.

That’s a bald-faced lie but Democrats don’t care and their media allies aren’t going to challenge them on those lies because they all want the same thing: Open borders so they can ‘fundamentally transform’ the current disgusting, racist, homophobic, intolerant, and far-too-white America.
