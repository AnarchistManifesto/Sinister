The United Nations International Panel on Climate Change (IPCC) has released their fifth report claiming that their scientists are 95% certain humans are the “dominant cause” of global warming since the 1950s, despite any evidence to the contrary.

Ninety-five percent is a ‘scientific certainty’ right? So nevermind the other 5%.

Nevermind the fact that the Climategate scandal revealed that major climate science players at the University of East Anglia’s Climate Research Unit had been skewing the data to fit the anthropogenic global warming agenda.

Nevermind the fact that a mini Climategate II emerged in the lead-up to this newest IPCC report’s release, as leaked documents showed that their scientists were struggling to make sense of cooling temps in the face of rising greenhouse gas emissions over the last fifteen years. They cannot explain the pause in warming, so they are just not going to.

Nevermind that the Mail Online is reporting “Leaked documents show that governments which support and finance the IPCC are demanding more than 1,500 changes to the report’s ‘summary for policymakers’. They say its current draft does not properly explain the pause.”

Nevermind that Earth System Science Center Director and Professor of Atmospheric Science Dr. John Christy analyzed all 73 UN computer models cited in the latest IPCC report and found there has actually been no statistically significant warming in the last 17 years.

Nevermind that the Arctic ice cap has grown a whopping 29% in just one year — with 553,000 more miles of ice-covered ocean than last year (despite the fact that the BBC reported back in 2007 that global warming would render the Arctic ice-free by this summer).

Nevermind that reports show the planet entered a period of global cooling in 1997 that isn’t expected to end until the middle of this century.

Nevermind that governments all over the world have funneled billions into so-called green measures based on the warnings that global warming would continue in a neverending J curve upward which is simply not true.

Nevermind that because the globe isn’t warming, the monicker ‘global warming’ was quietly replaced with ‘climate change’ because there’s no way to argue with the fact that the climate does indeed change the same way the wind is always blowing somewhere.

Nevermind the fact that, prior to Climategate II, a leaked UN report from earlier this year featured their scientists admitting that solar activity — something no government on Earth can control — may play a significant role in global warming.

Nevermind that scientists have known about this significant solar role since at least the 1960s as illustrated by this snippet from Popular Mechanics magazine printed in 1963:

This 1963 Popular Mechanics article summary discusses NASA satellites confirming in 1961 the Earth’s outer atmosphere changes density based on the solar cycle.

Nevermind that solar activity is currently at a 100-year low, and according to the Popular Mechanics article, “As solar activity diminishes, the upper atmosphere cools and shrinks.” Forget the fact that this has led some scientists to actually start researching whether or not Earth is currently headed toward another mini ice age.

Nevermind that proponents of anthropogenic climate change cannot carbon tax the sun, so it looks like they will stick their manipulated global warming scam to us, data be damned.
