“Total confusion”: Nicolas Bay on the objectives of France in Syria

News

Written by: 0x000216

May 13, 2018

The Vice President of National Front Nicolas Bay, who first came to the Yalta Economic Forum in Crimea, told Sputnik about his country’s role in the Syrian chessboard, particularly after having participated to the American-British made missiles. According to him, by joining the strikes carried out by Washington and London against Damascus, Paris has aligned itself with the American position “very warlike”, which is not consistent with the political and diplomatic tradition of France.

Thus, Paris has “sent a message that is harmful to the peoples of France, Europe and Syria”, and this in a period when the main objective is to unite against Daesh and other Islamist groups:

“He [Emmanuel Macron, editor’s note] finally gave way to a war-show without real military effectiveness, which he recognized himself, and for the sole purpose of designating the regime of Bashar al-Assad as an enemy. He said on the sidelines of the Economic Forum. “And in this context we must join forces with Russia and develop a military partnership with the Syrian army to overcome Islamist groups. I think this is a very bad signal and it is also a sign of distrust of Russia, whereas Russia should be considered as a great neighbor and friend. “

Here, a disparity: on the one hand the French government says it wants to establish a dialogue with its Russian counterparts, but on the other, it takes part in the strikes against Damascus. This demonstrates in fact that Emmanuel Macron lacks “a real strategy to defend our interests” and adds nothing to clarity in the French position, said Mr. Bay.

“Wishing to restore relations with Russia, then aligning with the US position in Syria in a second time, I think it gives a sense of total confusion and inconsistency of the French position, it undermines the credibility from France.”

One possible remedy for this blurred situation would be “a change in political orientation” which does not seem to meet the current stage for the interests of any of the peoples concerned.

“Instead of considering the question of Middle Eastern countries solely on the basis of moral consideration of the regimes that are in place, we should think together about the interests of our peoples. That is, did the French intervention in Libya comply with the interests of France? The answer is no, it caused massive waves of immigration. Is it consistent with the interests of Libyans? No, because it was the Islamists who benefited from it. Today in Syria it’s the same thing: is it in line with the interests of France? No, France must have in countries that are close to the European Union anyway, it must be areas of stability. And is it consistent with Syrian interests? No, because here again it is the Syrians who are under the yoke of Islamist groups. “

Therefore, one can find “a similarity in the facts: the situation in Syria is the reproduction of the political mistakes that were committed in Libya by France and the British, and in Iraq by the British and the Americans”, put highlight Sputnik’s interlocutor.

As for the possible consequences of these errors, Nicolas Bay listed several of them.

“The consequence is first of all to allow the Islamists to continue to deploy, to prosper. It is also a signal of laxity towards the powers that support Islamist factions. I think of Qatar, Saudi Arabia who are now the beneficiaries of this situation. So the ambiguous position of France, hostile to the regime in power in Damascus, obviously strengthens the countries that finance and support Islamists such as Saudi Arabia.

At the same time, on the scale of the main European chancelleries, very harsh words targeting Russia have followed the poisoning of the former double agent Sergei Skripal, and this “without any control, without any verification, without any real investigation” . For Mr. Bay, this campaign follows the logic of a cold war against Russia. However, the main interest of Paris would be a de-escalation, which is still not put in place by the President of the Republic, even months after his election, he lamented.

“He is completely subject to American and British positions. And this is very damaging because I think that France, its geographical position initially by its history, too, could be a balance should take a balanced position in the relationship between Russia and the countries of Europe, also between Russia and the United States. “

Finally, referring to the undesirable prospect that extremist groups implanted one day an autonomous state with the help of the USA in the south of Syria, the vice president of the FN said that the analysis of the Russian Ministry of Foreign Affairs finds “validated by the facts”.

“The fact of having in Syria the Islamic State which is not yet totally destroyed on the one hand and on the other hand the Islamist groups mainly supported by the Saudis who are beneficiaries of the strikes of the United States against Syria, it results in allowing these Islamist groups to prosper, to settle, and so even if it is not the initial objective, it is the result in any case of the policy led by the United States ” he summarized.
