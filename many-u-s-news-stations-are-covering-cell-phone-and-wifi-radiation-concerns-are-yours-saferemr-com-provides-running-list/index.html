By B.N. Frank

Even though tech companies continue marketing their products to people of all ages, many local and national news stations have been airing segments about cell phone and wireless WiFi radiation exposure concerns for several years already.

Some Americans may have first become concerned only because celebrities and public figures like Dr. Oz, Dr. Jill Stein, Kourtney Kardashian, Suzanne Somers, and more have used their platforms to talk about this.  Many documentaries have also been produced including Generation Zapped.

Dr. Joel Moskowitz of University of Berkley, CA is one of many American scientists who has also been trying to raise awareness.  His SaferEMR.com website includes one page devoted to U.S. news stations airing segments about exposure concerns.

Many other media sources have also been contributing articles and segments about cell phone and wireless WiFi radiation concerns.  Some are referring to The Telecom Industry as “Big Wireless” and are comparing them to “Big Tobacco.”

Everyone over the age of 40 understands this reference  People used to be allowed to smoke everywhere –airplanes, doctors’ offices, hospitals, and schools.  Tobacco marketing campaigns were allowed to be directed at kids until the late 1990s.

New technology can be wonderful when used in moderation.  Many of us thrived using it this way when it was originally introduced. 

Now “Big Wireless” seems to be intent on inventing and marketing more stuff to every single one of us – from cradle to grave – They make and market stuff that’s not safe or healthy and promote it as safe and healthy.  They make and market stuff we don’t need.   They intentionally make stuff that violates our privacy, is addictive and has short life-spans.

“Big Wireless” may even be powerful and ruthless than “Big Tobacco.”

“Big Wireless” has already taken over many of our medical facilities, libraries, schools, school buses, and public transportation systems.

Current marketing campaigns include automation that will eliminate millions of jobs,  Artificial Intelligence (AI), “The Race for 5G,” “Smart Cities,””Smart” Infrastructure,” “The Internet of Things (IoT),”and  WiFi “hotspots”

For several years already, wireless and digital utility “Smart” Meters have proved to be a colossal failure and have cost taxpayers billions of dollars.  However they are still being marketed as beneficial to customers, “energy efficient,” and “eco-friendly.”

All of this new technology is contributing to Electronic Pollution (Electrosmog) and Electronic Waste (E-Waste).

Decades of research has proven this is all biologically and environmentally harmful.  It’s not just about cancer.

Unfortunately, “Big Wireless” can do whatever they want because of outdated regulations and guidelines written 20 years ago.  Current local, state and federal officials are creating and passing new laws giving “Big Wireless” more power to force their products on communities and residents.

“The Precautionary Principle” is being ignored.  Scientists are being paid to defend outdated guidelines and regulations.  Besides biological and environmental health consequences, failed projects are costing taxpayers A LOT of money.

NBC Nightly News used to produce segments for a series they called “The Fleecing of America.”  NBC News is long overdue for another story on “Big Wireless.”

If your local news stations aren’t producing and airing programs about cell phone and WiFi radiation as well as Electrosmog, maybe you should contact them and ask them why.
