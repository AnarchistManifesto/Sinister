Oh by the way, I should point out that HRSA’s report states that under Injury the total is 13,964! Doesn’t that number warrant an investigation too?

There’s one more thing I think Congress and readers ought to know. The GM problem was ‘hidden’ for ten or so years. Again, that’s nothing compared with what the U.S. CDC/FDA has done about hiding autism being related to or caused by mercury in vaccines. That conspiracy goes back to 1999 when the CDC’s epidemiologist Dr. Thomas Verstraeten found that ethylmercury in vaccines increased the risk of subsequent development of neurologic development impairment. See the quote taken from the FOIA request a member of Congress received in August 2013, i.e., the CDC response (File 10 25 of 334):

Conclusion: This analysis suggests that high exposure to ethyl mercury from thimerosal-containing vaccines in the first month of life increases the risk of subsequent development of neurologic development impairment, but not of neurologic degenerative or renal impairment. Further confirmatory studies are needed. [1] [CJF emphasis added] [which apparently led to the Simpsonwood Meeting that I talk about in my book Vaccination Voodoo.] [3]

Here’s the smoking gun and clincher taken from that Verstraeten study paper:

Results: We identified 286 children with degenerative and 3702 with developmental disorders, and 310 with renal disorders. The relative risk (RR) of developing a neurologic development disorder was 1.8 (95% confidence intervals [CI] ::: 1.1-2.8) when comparing the highest exposure group at 1 month of age (cumulative dose> 25 μg) to the unexposed group. 

Within this group we also found an elevated risk for the following disorders: autism (RR 7.6, 95% Cl = 1.8-31.5), non organic sleep disorders (RR 5.0, 95% Cl = 1.6-15.9}, and speech disorders (RR 2.1, 95% (1=1.1-4.0). For the neurologic degenerative and renal disorders group we found no significantly increased risk or a decreased risk. [2] [CJF emphasis added]

CDC definitely knew ethylmercury caused “elevated risk” for autism because Dr. Verstraeten reported “autism (RR 7.6, 95% Cl = 1.8-31.5). And yet, that devastating and life-altering information was withheld from the public while Verstraeten reworked that incriminating data after the CDC/FDA and vaccine makers’ secret pow-wow in June 2000 at the Simpsonwood Conference Center in Georgia.

Here’s what Dr. Verstraeten had to say at that Simpsonwood Meeting taken from the transcript:

“We have found statistically significant relationships between exposure [to mercury in vaccines] and outcomes. At two months of age, developmental delay; exposure at three months, tics; at six months, attention deficit disorder. Exposure at one, three and six months, language and speech delays—the entire category of neurodevelopmental delays.” – Tom Verstraeten, U.S. CDC Epidemiologist —Simpsonwood Meeting transcript [4]

And here are other remarks taken from that transcript.

“I have to say the number of kids getting help in special education is growing nationally and state by state at a rate not seen before. So there is some kind of increase. We can argue about what it is due to.”

“I have taken a lot of histories of kids who are in trouble in school. The history is that developmental milestones were normal or advanced and they can’t read at second grade, they can’t write at third grade, they can’t do math in the fourth grade and it has no relationship as far as I can tell to the history we get of the developmental milestones. So I think this is a very crude measure of neurodevelopment.”

“To think there isn’t some possible problem here is unreal.” – Dr. William Weil, a pediatrician representing the Committee on Environmental Health of the American Academy of Pediatrics — Simpsonwood Meeting, June 2000 [4]

“But there is now the point at which the research results have to be handled, and even if this suspect that it is already too late to do anything regardless of any professional body and committee decides that there is no association and that information gets out, the work has been done and through the freedom of information that will be taken by others and will be used in other ways beyond the control of this group. And I am very concerned about that …”
