Latest Trump ‘golden shower’ NON-scandal proves that ‘mainstream’ journalists have lost their bearing, their purpose and their minds

Liberty

Written by: 0x000216

May 14, 2018

(NationalSentinel) It’s no secret that the Leftist ideologues who laughingly claim to be ‘impartial’ reporters and journalists in the ‘mainstream’ media have it in for President-elect Donald J. Trump. They made their disdain for him perfectly clear during last year’s presidential campaign, by inventing narratives, creating straw men and outright accusing Trump of being everything but a human being.

But what has happened over the last 24 hours in the media as it pertains to the president-elect is not just bizarre, it borders on the psychopathic.

As noted by Zero Hedge, some goofball literally invented a story about how Trump supposedly hired a bunch of Russian hookers to put on a “golden shower” show at a hotel being monitored by Russian intelligence (for the uninitiated, ‘golden shower’ refers to the act of urinating on someone, as some sort of deviant sexual act):

In a story that is getting more surreal by the minute, a post on 4Chan now claims that the infamous “golden showers” scene in the unverified 35-page dossier, allegedly compiled by a British intelligence officer, was a hoax and fabricated by a member of the chatboard as “fanfiction”, then sent to Rick Wilson, who proceeded to send it to the CIA, which then put it in their official classified intelligence report on the election.

These ridiculous claims, along with the pages of other allegations, rumors and unsubstantiated claims were picked immediately by some of the usual suspects in the mainstream media. As reported by The Daily Wire, they picked up this nonsense up and ran with it, :

Just as we saw last Friday, with the completely fact and evidence-free dog and pony show put on by the Intelligence Community and their media allies, late Tuesday afternoon, the Establishment Empire struck again, this time with an unprecedented double down. No doubt working in tandem (and with Donald Trump’s many establishment enemies on both the left and right), CNN and BuzzFeed did something so sleazy and dishonest that, just a few years ago, even to a media cynic like me, it would have seemed impossible.

With a breathless breaking news report, left-wing CNN went first. To no one’s surprise, the once-respected Jake Tapper took the lead. Undoubtedly CNN’s overall goal was to offer up the imprimatur of “journalism” so that a batch of leaked and completely unsubstantiated anti-Trump documents could be dumped on the American public the night before the President-elect’s very first press conference.

Lifezette added:

The online news site BuzzFeed on Tuesday published a letter containing salacious allegations — which — against President-Elect Donald Trump.

The letter, purporting to come from a retired British intelligence agent, details Trump’s alleged relationship with Russia and contends that the Kremlin has been “cultivating, supporting and assisting” Trump for at least five years.

Buzzfeed editor-in-chief Ben Smith, who has long sank to the level of hack, actually defended publishing unsubstantiated claims and rumors about a president-elect, his disdain for Trump being that pronounced.

“Our presumption is to be transparent in our journalism and to share what we have with our readers,” he wrote in the email he subsequently shared on Twitter. “We have always erred on the side of publishing. In this case, the document was in wide circulation in the highest levels of American government and media.”

Back who you want politically, but there is one hard and steadfast rule in journalism: Be absolutely certain of your facts before you publish them, especially when you’re writing about someone who is preparing to become the leader of the free world.

“Despite the fact that these documents are unsubstantiated, despite the fact that — and this is important — the rabidly anti-Trump media has had almost a year to substantiate them and could not,  by pretending it had a legitimate news story about the documents, CNN laid the groundwork for BuzzFeed‘s Ben Smith to violate every code of ethics in the world of journalism,” writes John Nolte at The Daily Wire.

He goes on to note that both CNN and Buzzfeed, among other mainstream outlets, have preached for months about the ‘evils’ of “fake news,” but have no problem publishing it when it comes to smearing Trump.

“As someone who has been sounding the alarm about BuzzFeed‘s Ben Smith for ten years, and about CNN for about three years, I can only say, told ya’ so. Again and again, I warned that both news outlets had long ago ceased to be anything close to legitimate; that there is no barrel bottom they won’t scrape, no lie they won’t tell, no dirty partisan trick they won’t pull in order to further their left-wing agenda and protect the rotting corpse of the failed status quo,” Nolte says.

He’s right, of course. These people have lost any sense of journalistic principle. And, some, their minds.
