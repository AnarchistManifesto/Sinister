Mother’s Day 5G Prayer for the Planet: Anke Kern, “Resonance Beings of Frequency” and “Generation Zapped”

World

Written by: 0x000216

September 7, 2019

By Patricia Burke

When there is a debate about the very nature of science, one way to dive deeply is to move beyond the narrative in the United States and to examine what is being reported in other countries.  German environmentalist Anke Kern recently gave a wide-ranging lecture on the danger posed to to the planet from man-made non-natural wireless telecommunications networks that are being promoted as “progress” as well as the solution to environmental and social challenges.

FROM GERMANY

The lecture by Anke Kern: “Digitalized into a radiant future – dead certain!” by KLA.tv is available in English below.

﻿

Never before has mankind been so erroneously informed concerning a health and environmental policy related issue – and with such a huge effort – through the good cooperation of the industrial sector, politics and the system media. This risky technology has reached all social classes, has made people dependent and addicted and they are screaming for more. It is the satanic stroke of genius of the dark forces on this planet earth during the past century.

So far they have never succeeded in making people eliminate themselves and sacrifice their own children on the altar of technology for their god, called progress.

In a way it is comprehensible that this industry itself is not interested in bringing to light the negative side of their products. But the fact that all social institutions are helping to keep this crime in the dark, just overshadows everything else. And I have dealt with environmental policy for more than 30 years. This experience has really deeply shaken my belief in the innate good in human beings. This industry sector can spare itself a big army of lobbyists, because their collaborators sit in school boards, in health agencies and are present in municipal and town councils.

Namely in 1968 the US government had been asked by scientists and physicians to realize a study about the electromagnetic environmental contamination. In 1968! And the report came out in 1971. And I quote this one sentence: Quote: “If in near future appropriate precautions and controls are not introduced, based on a fundamental understanding of the biological effects of electromagnetic radiation, in the coming decades the human world will enter an era of ecological contamination by energy, which can be compared to the chemical contamination of the environment of today”.
