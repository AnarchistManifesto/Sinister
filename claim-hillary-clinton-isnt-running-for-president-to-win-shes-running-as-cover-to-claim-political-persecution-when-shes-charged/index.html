(National Sentinel) Cover Thy Backside: Following POTUS Donald Trump’s replacement of Attorney General Jeff Sessions earlier this month with his chief of staff, Matthew Whitaker, you may have noticed there was a sense of panic on the Democratic Left.

Almost immediately, leading Democrats and their lapdogs in the compliant establishment media lashed out at the president over his decision, claiming without any evidence or cause that Whitaker, like Sessions before him, must recuse himself from special counsel Robert Mueller’s never-ending investigation into criminal collusion between the 2016 Trump campaign and Mother Russia.

The superficial ‘explanation’ for Democrats’ angst is that Whitaker is an open critic of the Mueller probe and has written about his disdain for the investigation. That, say Democrats, is why he cannot be trusted to fairly administer the probe he has now been put in charge of.

But is there something else behind the Democratic opposition to Whitaker’s appointment and oversight of the Mueller investigation? What is really driving Democratic panic?
