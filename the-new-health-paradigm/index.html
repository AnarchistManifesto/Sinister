If you ask the average person to list some “healthy” foods, they may detail a variety of fruits and vegetables, or perhaps assorted whole grain products. Some individuals may even add dairy products to the list. The truth of the matter is that, given the nature of our modern society, this kind of thinking is no longer valid.

Thanks to the negative byproduct of scientific advancements, you simply cannot judge the health properties of a given food product based on your preconceived notion of the food being ‘healthy’.

Traditionally healthy food items such as conventional vegetables, prominently pushed as the healthy choice in grocery stores and by conventional wisdom alike, now contain dirty secrets that render them completely unhealthy.

A number of invisible threats to your health that are lurking incognito in the food supply include:

  • Genetically modified ingredients (GMOs): linked to sterility, infant mortality, cell death, and the potential destruction of the planetary biosphere.
  • Artificial sweeteners (aspartame, sucralose): Found to spawn tumors in the majority of female rats tested. The study showed that of 48 rats experimented on, up to 67 percent of all female rats developed tumors roughly the size of golf balls or larger.
  • Monosodium glutamate (MSG) (in various forms to be listed): an obesity-inducing “flavor enhancer” that has an enormously sweet taste and is widely used by the restaurant industry to make otherwise bad and unhealthy food taste delicious.
  • High-fructose corn syrup: known to contain mercury, promotes obesity more than regular sugar, and is prevalent among the food supply.
  • Pesticides: pesticides are known to be causing birth defects and are also contributing to the obesity epidemic.
  • Herbicides: herbicides like Roundup are causing mental illness, obesity, killing human kidney cells, and killing the butterfly population.
  • Pre-sale irradiation: a large amount of produce is subject to pre-sale irradiation (including ionizing gamma radiation, the kind used in nuclear material and and X-rays).
  • Bisphenol-a (BPA): found to be leading to infertility, diabetes, depression, and even cancer.

Even if you are familiar with all of these nutritional dirty bombs, there is a significant chance that you are still being tricked into consuming them through deceptive labeling and the selective omission of ingredients. The food industry can be extremely slimy when it comes to telling you the truth. The truth of the matter is that many major brand names will do almost anything in order to conceal the truth behind health-destroying ingredients that lurk inside your favorite products.

The old notion of ‘healthy’ has been thrown out the window thanks to these hazardous substances and fillers. With this, comes the emergence of a new health spectrum — a new health paradigm.
