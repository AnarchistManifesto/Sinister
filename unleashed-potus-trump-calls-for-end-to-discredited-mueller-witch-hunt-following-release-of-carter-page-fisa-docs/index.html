(National Sentinel) Shut It Down: Over the weekend the Justice Department finally handed over some 400-plus pages of documents related to a year-old Freedom of Information Act request by Judicial Watch.

The government watchdog has been trying to get documents related to the FBI’s Foreign Intelligence Surveillance Act court application to spy on minor 2016 Trump campaign figure Carter Page.

Already we learned that FBI counterintelligence official Peter Strzok, who was in charge of investigating the Hillary Clinton classified email scandal as well as the probe into the Trump campaign, may have perjured himself in testimony before Congress.

Strzok said he had nothing to do with the Page FISA warrant applications, but it turns out he did.
