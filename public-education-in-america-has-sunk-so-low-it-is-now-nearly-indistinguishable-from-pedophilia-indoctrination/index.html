Public education in America has sunk so low it is now nearly indistinguishable from pedophilia indoctrination

World

Written by: 0x000216

September 7, 2019


By J. D. Heyes

(NationalSentinel) Thanks to the Left’s multi-decade campaign to completely take over the American primary education system, the “pop culture,” the entertainment industry, and the Washington federal bureaucracy, the mainstreaming of what used to very obviously be considered ‘deviant’ behavior is nearly complete.

The Left’s adult infatuation with children — sexually — is a case in point. For some reason, as political commentator Jon Bowne observed recently, “The emancipation of the hardcore lifestyles of sexual deviants has become a political priority in America.”

And, of course, not just for adults. These lifestyles have gained a strong foothold in our education system, as evidenced by course work now being forced upon our children at a very young age.

“An entirely new phase of pedophiles have carved out a social media space where they are fighting for acceptance…while magazines like Teen Vogue offer teenage girls articles legitimizing the sex trade industry,” Bowne notes.

Beginning with a push to legitimize and even promote homosexuality in the classroom, the extreme American Left has moved into the promotion of the so-called “LGTBQ” lifestyle, actually pushing children to ‘self-identify’ as the opposite sex and to push back hard against any attempt by rational adults to fight it.

Leftists in Congress — who have hijacked the Democrat Party — are leading the push. In recent days, for example, Sen. Doug Jones (D-Ala.), noted on the chamber floor that “there is still work to be done with regard to gay rights,” especially LGBT rights, and this, despite the fact that lawmakers have passed, and presidents have signed, civil rights legislation mandating the equal treatment of all Americans successively since the 1960s. So what, at this point, ‘remains to be done?’

Answer: To mainstream deviancy, not simply tolerate it.

In February, The Federalist reported that kids as young as seven are “next on the sexual revolution’s hit parade,” noting:

“Activists for normalizing pedophilia are on the move. Public acceptance of adult sex with children is the next domino poised to fall in identity politics. It’s being sustained, among other things, by the rapid sexualization of children in the media and in K-12 education,” wrote Stella Morabito for the site.

She notes that the “groundwork” for normalizing adult sex with children is already being laid, and that if our society isn’t “prepared,” we’re going to be blindsided by it. The Left is pulling out all the propaganda stops, infiltrating the medical community, Congress, the courts, the education establishment, and pop culture.

Consider the push to have drag queens — men who dress as women — read stories to children via “Drag Queen Story Hour” events at public libraries and other venues that are otherwise child-friendly. As NewsTarget reported in April, an increasing number of these events are drawing in convicted sex offenders and pedophiles, which, of course, was entirely predictable.

In December, Natural News reported that the creator of Drag Queen Story Hour actually admitted he was “grooming” kids to become transgender “queers” and drag queens.

“I’m here to let you know that this event is something that’s going to be very beautiful and for the children and the people that supported are going to realize that this is going to be the grooming of the next generation,” the creator, Dylan Pontiff, was quoted as saying. “We are trying to groom the next generation to not see the way that they just did.”

Efforts to further propagandize this aberrant behavior will be met with the same ‘resistance’ formula the Left has used — successfully — during each ‘defining deviancy down’ campaign: Portray those who are critical as out-of-step, archaic, and clinging to an era of bygone morality.

Remember: This began with sex education in high school, curricula that has since spread to junior high classes as well. Most American parents are at least tacitly okay with it, too, as Morabito noted.
