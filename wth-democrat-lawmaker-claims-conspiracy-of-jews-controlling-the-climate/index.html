WTH? Democrat lawmaker claims conspiracy of “Jews controlling the climate”

Liberty

Written by: 0x000216

May 15, 2018


By J. D. Heyes, editor-in-chief

(National Sentinel) Lunacy: As of this writing, another major winter storm — a “Nor’easter,” as they are called along the East Coast — was set to dump more than a foot of snow in the region, with some parts expected to get 16 inches or more.

“Another major storm will spread wet snow and travel disruptions from parts of Kentucky and Ohio to coastal New Hampshire and Maine as spring begins,” AccuWeather reported.

Added the UK’s Daily Mail:

The third Nor’easter, which arrived on March 12, mostly affected Connecticut and Long Island.

More than 18 inches were recorded in Southampton and 11 inches in Newtown during the third storm.

The week prior, Winter Storm Quinn dropped several inches of wet snow on New England and Philadelphia to New York City. 

Millions of residents were left without power and others were forced the cancel travel arrangements.

What’s going on? Why are all of these ‘unusual’ storms striking the Northeast? Is this the latest evidence of “human-caused global warming?”

Sure is, according to a local Washington, D.C., lawmaker, Democrat Trayon White, Sr.

But not just any humans. Humans who are Jews are responsible for this drastic, dangerous weather.

As reported by Breitbart News, White made his ridiculous, anti-Semitic and bigoted false claim in a video posted on his Facebook page (which, at the time Breitbart’s story was published remained online).

“Man, it just started snowing out of nowhere this morning, man. Y’all better pay attention to this climate control, man, this climate manipulation,” White warned in his video message. “And D.C. keep talking about, ‘We a resilient city.’ And that’s a model based off the Rothschilds controlling the climate to create natural disasters they can pay for to own the cities, man. Be careful.”

Um….okay.

As Breitbart noted further:

The Rothschilds are a wealthy business dynasty descended from Mayer Amschel Rothschild, a famous Jewish banker who got his start in the mid-1700s. Due to the family’s vast success, wealth, and business power, the Rothschilds are regularly smeared by anti-Semitic conspiracy theories, the usual anti-Jewish tropes about the controlling the world’s wealth, starting wars, etc.

After posting this racist nonsense, White was confronted — sort of — by a reporter from the Washington Post. He responded with this: “I work hard every day to combat racism and prejudices of all kinds. I want to apologize to the Jewish Community and anyone I have offended. The Jewish community have been allies with me in my journey to help people. I did not intend to be anti-Semitic, and I see I should not have said that after learning from my colleagues.”

Everything White said is 100 percent correct (though I’m not sure how much help the Jewish community has given him in his “journey to help people,” but it’s a safe bet it will be a lot less in the future). What he said was anti-Semitic. It was offensive. He shouldn’t have said it — not just because it was bigoted but because it was ridiculous on its face.

That said, I won’t deny that weather control isn’t a real thing. After all, there are real companies that do it, such as this one. But to insinuate that rich people of a certain ethnicity have some sinister control over the Nor’easters raking the upper Northeast is not just absurd, it’s divisive and dangerous, because — as Breitbart noted — it feeds into the worst lies and misunderstandings that Jewish people have long endured.

Kind of like black people such as Mr. White have endured centuries of cruel, damaging, and false stereotypes.

I keep hearing a lot about how wonderful and positive our “diversity” is, but at the same time I keep seeing racist, bigoted people of all skin colors and ethnicities use it as a socio-political weapon to keep us divided, and in the most farcical ways.
