Previous sex offenders continue to participate in ‘Drag Queen Story Hour’ in public libraries, thanks to Left-wing lunacy

Liberty

Written by: 0x000216

August 27, 2019



By Jon Dougherty

(NationalSentinel) You have to wonder why some parents would even allow their very young, very impressionable children to attend an event where a man dressed as a woman reads them stories, but it’s not so hard to figure out why perverts would be attracted to such events.

And yet, for some reason, the ‘woke’ of Austin, Texas, and elsewhere in far-Left, deep blue cities, continue allowing the two to come into close proximity with each other.

As reported by The Epoch Times, a pro-traditional family group has discovered that the Austin Public Library has permitted at least three sex offenders to participate in “Drag Queen Story Hour” over the past several months, “programs that seek to teach children about gender fluidity and give them queer role models through reading events in public libraries,” the news organization noted, adding:

David Richardson, known as “Miss Kitty Litter,” was arrested for prostitution in 1996, Mass Resistance, a pro-family organization based in Massachusetts, reported. After the group exposed the criminal record on Aug. 8, Austin’s LGBT Committee passed a resolution seeking to protect the story hour and the creation of more programs like it. Drag queens are men who dress up in women’s clothes while often wearing exaggerated makeup, usually for entertainment purposes.

So, drag queens have been around forever. Got it. But as a society we never let them near our kids. Until now.

“Can you imagine that? Arthur Schaper, the organizational director of Mass Resistance, told The Epoch Times last week. “The powers that be in Austin seem determined to attack us for wanting to alert the public about a sex criminal around children,” suggesting the committee’s move is a response against the push to cancel story hours.

The discovery of Richardson’s previous sex record is tame, though, compared to other drags who have been ‘outed’ as child sexual predators. According to criminal records obtained by Mass Resistance, William Travis Dees, a.k.a. “Liza Lott,” has participated in Drag Queen Story Hour at other Texas libraries. He was convicted in 2004 of sex-related crimes against four children between the ages of four and eight, and he’s listed as a “high risk sex offender.” But because he was 16 years old when convicted, his record was “sealed,” the group found out earlier this year.

Last September, another child sex offender participated in a story hour at the Houston Public Library:

Albert Alfonso Garza, 32, was found guilty of aggravated sexual assault of an 8-year-old boy in Texas on Sept. 9, 2009. The library released an apology statement on March 15, explaining that they “failed to complete a background check as required by [its] own guidelines.”

The Austin public library, unlike its Houston counterpart, does not perform background checks, however. It’s like officials there just don’t want to know.

The Epoch Times noted further:

Schaper said that this goes beyond criminal history. Some participants have been documented by his team to be publicly immersed in lewd and perverse behavior—including Richardson.

“Any school teachers who posted such sexually explicit content on social media would be immediately fired, but the library invites these people in to read,” the organization said in an exposé. Schaper added that despite their behavior online, they are presented as role models to children.

As for content, children are taught about the psychological alteration of gender during these story hours, a subject that public school students won’t learn until sixth through eighth grade, according to the National Sexuality Education Standards.

“The standard that is set for teachers, and people in school systems, should really be applied to anybody that deals with children, in a teaching mode,” Dr. Quentin Van Meter of Van Meter Pediatric Endocrinology told The Epoch Times. “And the Drag Queen Story Hours are advertised to teach children.”

In April, NewsTarget reported that Dees “is a current member of the ‘Space City Sisters’ drag queen group. He’s also repeatedly volunteered as a ‘greeter’ for young children at various DQSH events, as well as assumed other roles at these events that allowed him close access to underage children.”

Also:

It’s important to keep in mind that, despite Dees’ criminal records having been sealed, his sexual deviance and abhorrent behaviors and lifestyle habits have been in plain view for a long time – including in a very recent photo that he and several other “drag queens” took outside of the Houston Public Library for a social media photo-op promoting DQSH.

In the photo, Dees is seen wearing a bizarre outfit bearing the colors of light blue, pink, purple, and white, which are indicative of transgenderism. His “friends” are depicted in the photo dressed as Catholic nuns as a sign of mockery, with one holding a rubber chicken, which in LGBT symbolism stands for pedophilia.
