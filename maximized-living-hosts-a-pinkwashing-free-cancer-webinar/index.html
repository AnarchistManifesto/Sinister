Maximized Living Hosts A “Pinkwashing Free” Cancer Webinar

Maximized Living will be hosting a live webinar at 7:00 p.m. EST on Oct. 30 titled “The Problem with Pink.”

This free event will explore information brought to light in the book “The Cancer Killers,” which was inspired by the story of Dr. Charles Majors, who successfully defeated Stage 4 brain, blood and bone cancers through all-natural methods. Dr. Majors will co-host the event alongside natural health expert and book co-author Sayer Ji, who is the founder of the widely-referenced health website GreenMedInfo.com

The webinar will highlight the pinkwashing tactics used by the breast cancer industry to obfuscate the obvious causes of cancer, in favor of convincing folks all they can do is to raise money for a future, pharmaceutical cure, as well as to promote intrinsically carcinogenic “early detection” technologies such as x-ray based breast screenings.

Also, the webinar will explore all-natural tactics people have successfully used to avoid a future cancer diagnosis, including the methods and mindset Dr. Charles Majors to cure himself of what conventional doctors told him was ‘incurable’ cancer.

The official declaration of the war on cancer began in the 1970s, yet today the disease affects more people than ever. We raise millions of dollars for cancer awareness and prevention each year, but cancer rates continue to skyrocket. Maximized Living proposes that a cost-efficient, all-natural path to combat and kill cancer already exists.

If you already have cancer or know someone who does, this webinar will show you resources that exist to fight the disease without resorting to debilitating chemotherapy and radiation treatments.

Dr. Ben Lerner, co-founder of Maximized Living and co-author of “The Cancer Killers,” explains: “The great news about cancer is that people are not as genetically programmed to develop it as once thought. It’s a lifestyle issue, not a genetic one. Science has shown that through some very simple steps, you can radically reduce your chances of getting cancer as well as dramatically increase your chance of overcoming it if you’re dealing with this disease already.”

The webinar, titled “The Problem With Pink,” will focus on a trio of important points:

  • How cancer awareness has taken the place of practical study and treatment.
  • How early detection screenings can increase rates of cancer
  • How you can work to prevent and even reverse cancer.
