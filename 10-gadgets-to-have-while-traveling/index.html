1 PowerBank: Ice works 5000mAh Portable Charger

Power banks are a must while you’re traveling. Luckily for you the people at Ice works thought this one through because this powerbank is not only slim and small, but it also has a larger capacity than most other powerbanks. There’s no need for external cables as it comes equipped with not one but two! The slim and small design saves you the hassle of making room for this in your pocket or your carry-on bag. One of the cables is for an iPhone and the other is for Android cell phones.

2 Ceramic portable water filter

Ceramic water filters are in all probability among the foremost fashionable variants of handy water filters around. The ceramic part of those devices proves to be effective in filtering naturally-sourced water. Don’t forget to read a fantastic list of Top 10 best Stun Guns!!

3 Headphones that cancel every noise

Noise-cancelling headphones can make any long-haul flight a lot more pleasant; replacing the noise of engines with Taylor Swift, Beethoven, or whomever you choose to listen to throughout your time spent at 30,000 feet. Additionally, if you’re in a city where there’s lots of road traffic or noise, they’ll help you relax once you’ve landed. They can be connected to your cell phone or laptop with the help of Bluetooth.

4 Compact Camera: Panasonic Lumix ZS100/TZ100

Why bother with the hassle of carrying a compact camera around with you when you can just take photos with your cell phone? Here’s why; This camera covers wide angles and the great thing about this camera is that you don’t have to be a professional photographer in order to use it. You can still be able to take photos from this like a pro because it has great quality and was basically designed for people who are not exactly experts at taking pictures, much like most of us when it comes to photography.

5 Video recording sunglasses Spectacles from Snapchat!

Another hit from Snapchat! Now, these glasses not only record your entire vacation and sync it with the memories feature but they block the sun. It’s a glorious time to be alive. You must have these while you’re traveling because they don’t cause any inconvenience as you were already going to take a pair of glasses with you, why not take the far more upgraded version? These glasses have to be one of the most fun things to have during vacation.

6 Ostrich pillow

Our neck pillow days are over my friends. Ali Ganjavian dreamt up The Ostrich Pillow; this is a napping revolution. This gadget can be used while you’re on the plane, on the road and of course while you’re in bed or your couch.You can get these online in all sorts of different designs, happy nappin’ travelers. The one thing different about this pillow is that it doesn’t just rest on your neck like normal traveling pillows, it covers your face and prevents light from getting in while you’re asleep.

7 Travel steam iron

This one is for business travelers; it is the size of a computer mouse. This small steam iron has a 420-watt motor and three fabric heating levels. Due to this device, you can iron silk, wool, cotton, and linen with ease. You can not only carry it in your suitcase but you can also hand-carry it and iron your vacation clothes anywhere anytime. You don’t have to worry about safety when it comes to this gadget because it is not powered by a battery and cannot activate itself in your bag while you are moving it around; you simply just plug it in like any other steam iron.

8 Instant photos

The Polaroid Snap Touch is a middle ground between old and new. It’s a digital camera, but one that can print your pictures straight away – or save them for editing if you are not happy with your first attempt. It’ll play nicely with your smartphone, yes, this camera CAN connect with your cell phone which is very convenient and makes taking pictures much easier! This Polaroid ends up somewhere in the middle between professional cameras and normal digital cameras with one upgrade that is being able to print the picture you take instantly!
