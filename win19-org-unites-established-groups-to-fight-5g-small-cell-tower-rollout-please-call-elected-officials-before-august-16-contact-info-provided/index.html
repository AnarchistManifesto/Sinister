WIN19.org Unites Established Groups to Fight 5G Small Cell Tower Rollout. Please Call Elected Officials before August 16. Contact Info Provided.

World

Written by: 0x000216

September 9, 2019

By B.N. Frank

The Wireless Information Network – Wif19.org – is

an online hub that provides access to the resources of non-profit organizations working to educate the public about the wireless issue, and links visitors to groups working on particular issues.

WIN19 is owned and maintained by Grassroots Environmental Education, Inc., a non-profit organization based in New York.

The campaign to stop the federal rollout of harmful 5G small cell towers and related infrastructure is in full force.  But everyone still needs to help to make this stop!

The Senate Commerce, Science and Transportation Committee will be meeting on August 16 to discuss federal legislation that eliminates municipal control in regard to small cell tower and related wireless infrastructure placement.  Contact information is provided in this article.

Many have already been fighting 4G and 5G small cell tower installation in their communities.  Because of this, legislation didn’t pass in some states like California.  Unfortunately it did pass in other states like Ohio where it has been in effect since August 1.

﻿

﻿

Many doctors, scientists, and organizations have been fighting legislation allowing the use of 5G technology including tribal and environmental groups.

Since late June, Dr. Naomi Wolf has been posting on social media about how residents of New York City and their pets are being harmed by 5G.

Exposure to 5G causes many health issues including profuse sweating.  The Federal Communications Commission (FCC) regulates wireless technology even though they are NOT a health organization.  FCC employees defer to the FDA to avoid being held accountable for harm.

﻿

So what’s The FDA doing about 5G?  So far they have approved the sale of prescription pads that reduce arm pit sweating.  Unfortunately there are awful side effects including shirts catching fire.  Other than that, they don’t seem motivated– even regarding research that has clearly proven harm from exposure to all sources of wireless radiation.

Since 2004, The International Association of Firefighters (IAFF) has opposed the use of stations for cell towers and antennas.  If firefighters are concerned about exposure – shouldn’t we all be?

5G and The Internet of Things (IoT) are risky and unnecessary.  Even high risk insurer, Lloyd’s of London, won’t cover wireless radiation hazards from new technology including cell towers.

For those who don’t want to live without 5G and IoT, they can buy a router and use it in their homes or businesses.  The rest of us shouldn’t be forced to have this in our communities.
