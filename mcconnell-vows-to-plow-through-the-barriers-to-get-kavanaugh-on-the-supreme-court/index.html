McConnell vows to ‘plow through’ the barriers to get Kavanaugh on the Supreme Court

Written by: 0x000216

(National Sentinel) Let’s Do It: Though earlier reports suggested that Senate Majority Leader Mitch McConnell might be wavering in his support for Judge Brett Kavanaugh, the Kentucky Republican assured the country on Friday that his nomination will move forward soon to a full vote of the Senate, Democratic distractions notwithstanding.

“We’re going to plow right through it,” McConnell said, The Gateway Pundit reported.

The Democratic propaganda machine, also known as the “mainstream” media, has been doing everything in their power to promote allegations leveled by Palo Alto professor Christine Blasey Ford who says that Kavanaugh nearly “raped” her 36 years ago — at some party…somewhere…on some date she can’t recall…with a guy in the room who says he wasn’t.

According to Fox News‘ congressional correspondent Chad Pergram, McConnell said, “In the very near future, Judge Kavanaugh will be on the Supreme Court…so keep the faith. We’re going to plow right through it.”
