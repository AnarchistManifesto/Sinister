WiFi Can Harsh Your Mellow by Disrupting Your Blood Brain Barrier

World

Written by: 0x000216

September 9, 2019

By B.N. Frank

Research has already determined that exposure to all sources of wireless radiation can disrupt the blood-brain barrier which can cause it to leak.  Sources include Bluetooth, cell phone radiation (1G, 2G, 3G, 4G, and 5G), and of course, WiFi.

﻿

This is why so many doctors, educators, parents, scientists are trying to have WiFi replaced with safer wired internet in schools and everywhere else children spend extended periods of time.  Silicon Valley parents have always been concerned about their own kids’ use and exposure to technology.  They are freaking out more than ever about this.  Since 2004, the International Association of Firefighters have opposed the use of their stations as base stations for cell towers and antennas.

﻿

﻿

Here’s what research on firefighters revealed about how exposure affected them:

**Note:  A pilot study was conducted in 2004 of six California fire fighters working and sleeping in stations with towers.  The study, conducted by Gunnar Heuser, M.D., PhD. of Agoura Hills, CA, focused on neurological symptoms of six fire fighters who had been working for up to five years in stations with cell towers. Those symptoms included slowed reaction time, lack of focus, lack of impulse control, severe headaches, anesthesia-like sleep, sleep deprivation, depression, and tremors.  Dr. Heuser used functional brain scans – SPECT scans – to assess any changes in the brains of the six fire fighters as compared to healthy brains of men of the same age.  Computerized psychological testing known as TOVA was used to study reaction time, impulse control, and attention span.  The SPECT scans revealed a pattern of abnormal change which was concentrated over a wider area than would normally be seen in brains of individuals exposed to toxic inhalation, as might be expected from fighting fires.  Dr. Heuser concluded the only plausible explanation at this time would be RF radiation exposure.  Additionally, the TOVA testing revealed among the six fire fighters delayed reaction time, lack of impulse control, and difficulty in maintaining mental focus.

Besides making the blood brain barrier leak, cell phone radiation (like alcohol) can also kill brain cells.  You may currently feel sharp as a tack and fully in control of your emotions; however, others may not be as fortunate as you – including your loved ones, your pets, and everyone else you encounter.  It’s possible that someday even you will be symptomatic to exposure – emotionally or otherwise.

﻿

So even though it seems to be legal for all sources of WiFi to be everywhere and used by everyone, that still doesn’t mean it’s safe or beneficial for it to be installed everywhere. It was once legal for people to smoke everywhere and doctors were even paid to advertise for tobacco companies.
