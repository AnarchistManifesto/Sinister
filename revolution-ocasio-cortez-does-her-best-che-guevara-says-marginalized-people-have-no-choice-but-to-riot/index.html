Revolution! Ocasio-Cortez does her best Che Guevara, says ‘marginalized’ people have ‘no choice but to riot’

World

Written by: 0x000216

September 7, 2019


(NationalSentinel) Beltway Republicans who still think that the socialist/Marxist faction of the Democratic Party has no influence and is just a sideshow need to do two things: Get out of the Beltway more, and start listening to the party’s rising stars.

Like Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez of New York.

Sure, there isn’t a week that goes by when she doesn’t massacre facts and say patently stupid and racist things, especially about whites and Jews. But her voice is resonating with the Millennial crowd, and let’s face it, the young will take over the reins of power someday.

One way or the other, it seems.

In typical Che Guevara fashion, Ocasio-Cortez called for a popular uprising — there’s no other way to characterize it — during a radio interview on Wednesday, which makes us wonder if she really understands what she’s saying or if she’s just mouthing the first words that come to her mind.

Revolucion, in other words.

Speaking to hip-hop radio host Ebro Darden of “Ebro in the Morning, the freshman socialist used the example of Israel and the Palestinians — of course — to call for “riots” in the streets, and to hell with the democratic process.

“Now you look around the globe and you have multiple corrupt governments working together — you’ve got Israel, you’ve got America, you’ve got Russia, you’ve got the Saudis, right — all working in concert,” Darden said, the Washington Examiner reports.

“And I bring up Israel because you’ve been vocal about Palestine, the occupation and what needs to go on there. And specifically, I even think people in Israel are trying to get rid of [Prime Minister Benjamin] Netanyahu as well,” the host continued.

Stop. First of all, let’s be clear: These people ‘bring up Israel’ because there is a rising, ugly anti-Semitism in the Democrat Party, which is less “democrat” and more “party” these days, as in the “Communist Party. And secondly, ‘people in Israel’ aren’t trying very hard to ‘get rid of’ PM Netanyahu because he was just reelected to become his country’s longest-serving leader.

But I digress.

“I agree, I agree with that,” AOC said, unsurprisingly.

Darden the anti-Semite wasn’t finished. ”

“How do you have white supremacist Jews?” he asked. “How do you have people like [White House adviser] Stephen Miller? How do you have these individuals who are legit aligning with racism and white supremacy, but they’re Jewish? And it’s something that most people can’t wrap their brains around. But it’s a real thing.

“And what’s going on with Israel and Palestine, while it’s very, very, deep, it is very, very, criminal, and it is very, very unjust.”

Yep, AOC agreed.

“Criticizing the occupation doesn’t make you anti-Israel, frankly. It doesn’t mean that you are against the existence of a nation,” she said.

In her case, and in the case of the other Democrat anti-Semites in Congress — the “Plus 3″ faction of AOC — yes, it does make them anti-Jewish. They’ve demonstrated that time and time again.

And she just did it again.

“It means that you believe in human rights, and it’s about making sure that Palestinian human rights are equal to Israeli human rights, and there are a lot of troubling things happening there,” Ocasio-Cortez continued. Yeah, and to hell with Israeli rights; when you come at the issue already having decided that one side is ‘guilty’ of ‘crimes,’ then there is no ‘discussion.’

But then she said…

“Jewish people have been persecuted throughout all of human history, but I don’t think that by marginalizing Palestinians, you create safety.

“I believe that injustice is a threat to the safety of all people, because once you have a group that is marginalized and marginalized and marginalized — once someone doesn’t have access to clean water, they have no choice but to riot, right?” she said. “And it doesn’t have to be that way.”

Mind you, ‘injustice’ in her mind is anything that Israel does to defend itself and to ensure its own survival. And Jews have been more than just “persecuted;” one man — a real fascist, not a made-up one like POTUS Trump, tried to wipe them all out less than 80 years ago.

But is she just talking about Israel? Nope.

“I’m not even talking about Palestinians,” she continued. “I’m talking about communities in poverty in the United States, I’m talking about Latin America, I’m talking about all over the world.”

“Social destabilization is what happens when people do not have a plan or feel like there’s no vision for their future,” Ocasio-Cortez added.

Got that, Republicans inside the Beltway? Because you happen to be poor — probably even as a result of Democrat urban policies — you have ‘right’ to riot.

This is the language of violent revolution. It’s the language of Left, and it’s how the Left has taken power in country after country since the modern Left was born in Czarist Russia after World War I.

And GOP, in case you missed the Democrat debates, the entire party is shifting to AOC’s way of thinking, not the other way around. Think not? Then ask yourself how a freshman member of Congress gets away with calling her party’s congressional leader, House Speaker Nancy Pelosi, a racist and then gets a meeting with her one-on-one?

This little girl may say stupid things and make racist comments all the time. But she’s being taken seriously by a large group of Americans who believe her BS.

If the GOP doesn’t start looking at the Democrats as the party of revolution — not as in our founding and break from Great Britain to establish a republic but in the mold of Guevara, Fidel Castro, and Lenin, to establish a nightmarish dictatorship — they’ll never hear the first shots coming.
