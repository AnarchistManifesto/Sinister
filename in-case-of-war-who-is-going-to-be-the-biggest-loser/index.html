In case of war, who is going to be the biggest loser

News

Written by: 0x000216

May 13, 2018

“Nobody wants a new war in the region, but if it is triggered, it will be the last, because Tel Aviv will not come out unscathed, just like what happened to Germany during the two world wars, “wrote the columnist  Rai al-Youm .

According to the famous journalist of the Arab world, most strategic analysts were unanimous on Tuesday to say that the show presented by Netanyahu on Monday had not brought any proof of the violation of the Iranian nuclear agreement since its entry into force in January 2016. .

Indeed, Netanyahu’s statements aim to mark the ground for an attack on Iran, the Iranian bases in Syria or the Iranian territory, and to find favor in the eyes of some Arab and European governments as well as the United States.

The latest developments in the sensitive region of the Middle East provide for a large military explosion.

On the one hand, Moroccan Foreign Minister Nasser Bourita announced on Tuesday the break-up of his country’s relations with Iran, after which his government expelled the Iranian ambassador and immediately closed the Iranian embassy to Iran. Rabat, under the pretext of the financial and military support of Hezbollah to the Polisario Front. This Rabat approach is just a pretext to encourage other Washington allies in the region to do the same. Indeed, Arab countries in the Persian Gulf basin have welcomed this decision from Morocco.

On the other hand, the new US Secretary of State Mike Pompeo called on Arab countries in the region to put aside their differences of opinion and focus on Iran. Abdel Bari Atwan adds that the Israeli regime has revealed the statements of Crown Prince Mohammed bin Salman in a meeting with Jewish leaders in the United States, according to which the Palestinian question is not a priority issue for Riyadh. focuses instead on the confrontation with “the influence of Iran in the region”.

In the current situation, the Europeans are worried that Netanyahu will succeed in involving the countries of the region as well as the United States in a new war whose spark will explode with the withdrawal of Washington from the nuclear agreement, it that is why Europe did not appreciate Netanyahu’s show in Qods on Monday that he claimed that the evidence before him showed that Iran had violated the nuclear deal and was conducting a secret nuclear program at the unknown to IAEA inspectors.

“Netanyahu is a professional liar. Although he is accused of corruption in Israel, however, liars like him are still welcome at US President Donald Trump and his military establishment that will go to war for Israel, “said Abdel Bari Atwan.

This is the war … And we have only to fasten our belts, in preparation for all possibilities … And we are convinced that Israel and its allies will come out the biggest losers …
