Four US Senators: Angus King (I-ME), Jim Risch (R-ID), Susan Collins (R-ME), and Martin Heinrich (D-NM) of the Senate Intelligence Committee have introduced legislation early in June of 2016 to protect the electric grid.  The bill is called “Securing Energy Infrastructure Act of 2016.” [6]   Interestingly, Josh del Sol, of Take Back Your Power [7], has summarized an important part below.

To keep the grid system from collapsing at the utility end from hackers, some federal legislators are proposing legislation to use analog equipment for grid security at the utility end. [CJF  emphasis added]

But that will leave everyone in between vulnerable!

So why is it that the New World Order folks, aka Agenda 21 upgraded to Agenda 2030, demand and get their surveillance devices—AMI smart meters—installed on most USA buildings, when, apparently, some members of the U.S. Senate finally are waking up to the fact that the entire grid can be taken down by two-bit hackers compromising individual smart meters on houses or any building that will cascade back onto the Internet of Things or the Grid?  But that’s not all!  Are readers ready for this?

European Union nations reject smart meters!

In a special report rendered, a senior European commission official admits that smart meter market penetration in the EU is still very low, especially in new member states, and there’s a big shortfall in investment!

Furthermore, “A transition to an intelligent electricity grid in Europe can take place without smart meters, industry players said, in comments that will embarrass the European Commission, which pushed a Europe-wide plan to roll out smart meters years ago.”  [8]

Additionally, “But progress has been sluggish, with few countries having completed their roll-outs and a number of nations – most notably Germany – having so far decided against a nation-wide deployment of smart meters.” [8]

With this “piece de resistance”: “And the countries that do have a commitment to smart meters, such as the UK, have run into hurdles in completing its roll-out because some meters would cease to work if a consumer decided to change energy supplier.” [8]

Don’t you just love how Europeans know how to implement resistance?  What’s wrong with ‘politically correct’ USA?  Have we lost our sense of ownership and given in to the New World Order agenda, allowing AMI smart meters to rule our lives, plus make us sick.

Getting back to the bill the Senators introduced, they seem to want to protect President Obama’s Executive Order 13636, Part 9 (a) dated February 12, 2013, “where a cybersecurity incident could reasonably result in catastrophic regional or national efforts on public health or safety, economic security, or national security.”

What in the name of common sense is wrong with them?  Where is their common sense?

Cybersecurity incidents can be prevented very easily by going back to analog meters for every utility, as “analog and non-digital control systems” is what the four Senators propose to protect the energy infrastructure!  Refer to 2 (A) under Sec. 3 Pilot Program for Securing Energy Infrastructure.
