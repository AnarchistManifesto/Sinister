In a just world, fired FBI Deputy Director Andrew McCabe wouldn’t be making the media rounds this week hawking an upcoming book that provides details into how he helped orchestrate an attempted coup against a duly elected president.

“I think it’s possible,” he responded. “I think that’s why we started our investigation. And I’m really anxious to see where [special counsel and former FBI] Director [Robert] Mueller concludes that.”
