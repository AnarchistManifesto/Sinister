America and the Nuclear Agreement

News

Written by: 0x000216

May 13, 2018

The US Secretary of State, who has just returned from Pyongyang, said the US House of Representatives is ready to go far beyond the lifting of sanctions to North Korea.

US Secretary of State Mike Pampo said at a news conference on Friday (May 11th) that if North Korea accepts early disarmament, the United States will help the country rebuild its economy.

The US Secretary of State for the meeting with the North Korean leader said he met with him and talked to Kim Jong-noton about complex issues, challenges and strategic decisions.

He returned to his country two nights ago with three American released prisoners from North Korea.

Also, media reported that Mr Pompeo had been in talks with North Korean officials to coordinate Donald Trump’s visit next month to Kim Jong-un.
President Donald Trump and North Korean leader Kim Jong-don are scheduled to meet in Singapore for another month.

On the other hand, the State Department spokesman has denied the promise of the Foreign Minister of the North Korea’s brighter future and providing financial assistance to the country.

The State Department spokeswoman, Heather Naveret, said in a Twitter message that this is a false report, Reuters reported. If North Korea discards nuclear weapons, they will have a bright future day, including international trade, investment, and the presence of tourists from all over the world.

All this while US President Donald Trump recently canceled the important Western nuclear deal with Iran, despite clear EU objections. Analysts believe the move by the US president will lead to a severe international distrust of the United States; the isolation of the United States in the world will cause a split between the country and its European allies and, in the end, will have a serious impact on the negotiations with North Korea.

In the same vein, George W. Bush warned the 42nd US president about the danger of isolating America from reaching an agreement with Iran, saying “the only way to achieve peace is through partnership and engagement.” There is nothing impossible, apart from each other. The result will be no failure and the risk of isolation. ”
Democrat Democrats Nancy Pelosi, formerly the House of Representatives, also saw the withdrawal of a nuclear deal with Iran as an American isolation from the global arena.
