Meeting in Trump Tower a few months before the 2016 presidential election

The US-based New York Times reported that Donald Trump Jr., senior son of US President, Donald Trump, had met with a special envoy from a Gulf state in Trump Tower a few months before the 2016 presidential election.

The New York Times said that the Special Representative for the Arab Princes at the meeting was George Nader, and according to Mr. Nader, the crown princes who lead Saudi Arabia and the United Arab Emirates are very keen on Donald Trump’s election victory and are ready to help in this way.

A visit to Tramp tower in New York was organized by Erik Prince, founder of private security company, Blackwater, according to the newspaper. In the meeting, a senior Israeli expert named Joel Zamel has also been present.

In the same vein, a company associated with Zamel intends to manipulate social networks for the benefit of tramp. Joel Zamel promises to Tramp’s son that he can steer cyberspace to Donald Tramp by opening countless bogus accounts.

After the election, Joel Zamel and George Nader went to the white house and met with Jared Kushner, the son-in-law and senior advisor to Tramp. The report states that the content and purpose of the meeting was “coping with Iran’s threat”.

George Nader met with senior security and military officials of Saudi Arabia and offered some specific proposals on how to deal with Iran.

In this regard, the report unveiled the efforts of individuals affiliated with the UAE and Saudi Arabia to promote a plan for economic sabotage in Iran and to create distrust among Iranian authorities. The purpose of these measures was to create an economic crisis in Iran and eventually overthrow the Islamic Republic of Iran.

Proving allegations published in this controversial report could trigger a new phase in the investigation of foreign interventions in the US presidential election and their role in winning the Tramp.

Whether the proposal was implemented to support Donald Trump in the presidential election, and the details of who was ordering the plan, is still in dispute. But Donald Trump Jr. was keen to hear from the meeting, and after this initial offer for help, Mr. Nader was immediately welcomed as a close ally by the Tramp headquarters advisers and repeatedly with the Jared Kushner, the son-in-law of Mr. Tramp and Michael Flynn, who later became the first National Security Advisor to the president.

It was reported, at the same time, Mr. Nader was touting for classified advertising plan to use private contractors to destabilize Iran, the regional foe of Saudi Arabia and UAE, respectively.

After Mr. Tramp’s victory, Mr. Nader paid a huge amount of money to Mr. Zamel, one of his relatives saying it was worth $ 2 million. About the reason for this payment there are a variety of narratives, including the fact that a Zamel affiliate has provided Mr. Nader with a detailed account of the important role of advertising on social networks in Mr. Tramp’s victory.

These meetings, which have never been reported before, can, in addition to Russia, open Saudi Arabia and the United Arab Emirates to the case of alleged affair between Donald Trump’s campaign headquarters and foreign governments and people.

Analysts consider Donald Trump’s political behavior about Iran and the US President’s infinite support for Saudi and Emirati policies in the region and the world as an important factor in improving the accuracy of the New York Times report.

Just 10 months after the meeting, Saudi Arabia and the United Arab Emirates cut off their ties with Qatar on charges of what they called “support for terrorism and cooperation with Iran,” in which Egypt and Bahrain participated.

During this political tension, Donald Trump obviously supported the Saudi bloc and accused Qatar of assisting terrorism. Although US Secretary of State, Rex Tillerson, has been trying to play the role of mediator in the crisis by styling the president’s position.
