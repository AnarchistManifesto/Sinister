Samsung Galaxy S4: An In depth Review of Features, Price and Specifications

Technology

Written by: 0x000216

May 14, 2018

Earlier, I wrote a preview on Samsung Galaxy S4, now phone has been finally launched and as usual has found some critics and some followers. But, before, going into depth on the story of critics and followers let’s have a brief look on to phone’s salient features.

The phone has been developed with a new lightning Samsung Exynos 5 processor. It’s a great S4 pro processor which provides great compatibility to the phone. The phone also has a stunning display having ability to show quality pictures up to 1080p, the screen of the phone is amazing a 5-inch AMOLED screen first time on display in the smartphone market.

The phone has been updated by latest version of Android 4.2.2, provides a user-interface which makes S4 a world-class phone. One of the outclass features of the phone is the cover in which the phone’s body is being wrapped. S4 is being wrapped in an ultra-thick jacket measuring only 0.31 meters.

Other features of the phone include a pedometer, automatic translation and a fascinating feature that is quite new in market naming wave-your-hands-in-the-air gestures and etc; let’s have a look into the features of this fascinating phone in a bit of depth.

Air gestures: This feature will allow the users to control the phone with their usual actions and that too without keeping a hand over the phone. Though, this would work only for limited apps by Samsung. But, would be quite fascinating for all the users.

Air View: This feature would allow the users to view the in depth information over certain pieces of content. Like, if a user puts his finger over the calendar he can have know-how of what can be expected of the day today.

Drama Shot: This feature can allow the users to shoot 12 pictures at a time and can show time-elapsed too.

Dual Camera: S4 allows its users to take rear and front snapshots at a time by opening both the cameras at a single time meaning the person taking snapshots can involve himself in the picture directly.

S Health: This is a pretty feature that will allow the users to keep a track record of their fitness levels. Samsung will soon be selling bracelets that would regularly check your blood pressure, glucose level, pulse rates and weight.

S Voice Drive: Feature, will allow drivers to control their phone through their voice while driving in their cars.

Samsung Smart Eye Pause: Tracks your eye movement if they aren’t on the screen the feature automatically stops anything running on the screen.

Samsung Smart Scroll: Allows you set scroll down any piece of content using the up and down movements of the phone.

Samsung WatchON: Allows your television to be controlled through your phone via an IR-sensor.

The phone also comes with a facility of 4G LTE and mutli-carrier. Samsung has build a might app store for there flagship phone. But, experts don’t rate the phone as great as it was predicted. As they believe the phone isn’t of the highest status as some of the earlier phone launched this year, in particular the Blackberry Z10. But, this isn’t convincing enough there are many followers of this phone which rate this phone really high, so, we would have to wait and watch what response this flagship phone get from the public.
