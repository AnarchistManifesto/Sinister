Democratic Socialist darling Ocasio-Cortez explains how she would BANKRUPT all Americans

World

Written by: 0x000216

September 9, 2019


(National Sentinel) Just Like Venezuela: The Democratic Party has been moving to the extreme Left for years, having really accelerated with Barack Obama’s election and eight years in office. Now, it appears as though the party has adopted socialism and Marxism as its official political philosophy, as we see more Democrats flock to the candidacy of Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez.

Promising free everything — higher education, healthcare, government assistance and guaranteed income — Ocasio-Cortez is developing quite a following, mostly from younger Americans swept up in the allure of getting without having to give.

But she hasn’t really discussed at length how she would pay for so much largess. Until this week.

In an interview with “The Daily Show,” host Trevor Noah played the devil’s advocate and asked Ocasio-Cortez how the country could afford to be handing out trillions and trillions of dollars.

Her rambling response says she would transform the United States into one giant Venezuela — massively in debt, dirt poor, destitute, and in chaos.

Where to start?

First of all, she makes it clear (as other Democrats have, including Democratic presidential hopefuls like Sen. Elizabeth Warren) she wants to take away your tax cuts and reimpose jobs-and-growth-killing taxes on American businesses. This — after the economy hit a modern-day record of 4.1 percent growth thanks in large part to POTUS Trump’s and the GOP’s tax cuts and regulation-killing.

Two, “folks paying their fair share” of taxes — meant to attack even moderately well-off earners — is the biggest lie Democrats have ever told. “The rich” don’t just pay most of the income taxes, they pay all income taxes. You know what’s not fair? Americans who use the same infrastructure and government services, and are protected by the same federal law enforcement and military — but don’t contribute to their funding. But you can’t divide Americans by class and socioeconomic status with that truth bomb, which is what socialists/Marxists do.

Three, a carbon tax would kill industry, period. It’s a pipe dream (and a lie) at this point to suggest we can force a transition to “clean” energy that is expensive and unproven. It’s just not feasible. Fossil fuels run economies. Take them away or tax them into oblivion and you obliterate economies.

Lastly, her claim that the military received “a $700 billion budget increase” is a lie. The budget has gone up under POTUS Trump (who campaigned on rebuilding our military and recapitalizing it), but not by $700 billion. Also, her claim that “the military” doesn’t want certain systems is cherry-picking; overall, if you polled military leaders, the answer would be universal that Trump’s budget increases are not only desired but needed.

So what kind of America would we be left with if the country were governed by Ocasio-Cortez and others like her?

One giant Venezuela. And while we were crashing and burning, so would the rest of the world.

This woman represents a dramatic shift in the Democratic Party. That should scare all of us.
