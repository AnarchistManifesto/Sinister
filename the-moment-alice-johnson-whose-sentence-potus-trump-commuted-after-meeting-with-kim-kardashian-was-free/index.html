The moment Alice Johnson, whose sentence POTUS Trump commuted after meeting with Kim Kardashian, was FREE

World

Written by: 0x000216

September 13, 2019


(National Sentinel) Freedom: A little more than a week ago Alice Johnson, 63, still believed she was going to serve many more years behind bars for a one-time non-violent drug offense.

She knew that reality TV star Kim Kardashian West had been to the White House to see President Donald Trump, to plead her case and ask the president for clemency on behalf of Johnson.

No doubt Johnson was very hopeful — who gets in to see the president of the United States, let alone someone on behalf of a citizen who’s in prison?

She had been sentenced to life in prison without any chance for parole.

Did she really have a shot at freedom? Those are just movie scripts, right?

Wrong.

Kardashian convinced POTUS Trump that Alice Johnson was most definitely worthy of a commutation. She had been convicted once of a drug-related offense that was non-violent and did not target others.

So on Wednesday late in the afternoon, Alice Johnson — who reportedly has a job waiting for her — was free once more.

The right thing for all Americans.
