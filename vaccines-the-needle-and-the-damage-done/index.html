I was also able to show how vaccines, in addition to being ineffective, are extremely dangerous as a result of their toxic ingredients. In conclusion, I suggested that the reason for the prevalence of toxic vaccinations and the false propaganda surrounding them was not only due to the greed of major pharmaceutical companies and vaccine makers but also to the desire of the elite to reduce the population and engage in a worldwide eugenics program.
